Corporate Events

Consider dance for your corporate, Christmas Parties, Summer Balls or Sports and Social Club events!

Guernsey Event Venues
Guernsey Salsa knows all the best venues for Guernsey corporate events and team building and is happy to arrange the most suitable venues on your behalf, aswell as menus - food, nibbles and/or drinks on arrival.

Corporate Gifts
Corporate gift ideas, personalized business gifts and flowers arranged. They can be placed on tables or handed out during the event.

Team Building - Guernsey
Dance Entertainment for Team Building and Corporate Events of all sizes and friendly events or dance lessons, increasing staff morale and team building during lunch breaks and sports and social club evenings, on site during office hours, evenings or weekends.

Dance Professionals
Three Salsa World Champions which Heidi Almonte has danced /performed with on numerous occasions are included in our 100 + professional dancers on our books. Including a variety of dance performers aswell as entertainers, from Guernsey or UK, to suit all budgets of corporate events and team building events.

Dance Lesson / Show
An easy Dance Lesson - Salsa, Latin, Ballroom or Burlesque - to start the corporate event or team building event, followed by dinner or tapas, a Show and Fun Dance Line Ups for team building - to fill the floor and set the party mood for a phenominal night (or day) out with a difference!

Salsa Bands
Lively addition to the entertainment. We know all the best Salsa Bands, including a fabulous Group who have even enjoyed joining guests on the dance floor and had one Bride and Heidi up on stage shaking their maracus!

Dance 'Taxis'
Dance 'Taxis' - (male and/or female) who will dance with guests, can also be arranged as a wonderful addition for memorable events and sports and social club team building.
