Frequently Asked Questions

Singles and couples of all ages welcome, you don't need to come with a partner and you will have the chance to dance with different people during classes.

Clothing
Cool clothing you can lift your arms up in easily. Layers if its cold - but you will soon warm up when dancing. Light coloured clothes keep you much cooler than black.

Jeans are great to show off ladies hip movement or dresses that fly out look fab when you spin!S

Dance Shoes
'Smooth bottomed' leather or dance shoes are easier to turn in, NOT trainers that grip the floor when you turn!
See selection of dance shoes for ladies and men in your dance class or contact Heidi.

Search for batchata, salsa or latin music on YouTube, Spotify or your preferred music provider.

Try: Fab Bachata Tracks = DJ Tronky, (DJ Khalid, Dj Soltrix, DJ John Moon), Prince Royce + search 'Bachata Version' Salsa = Marc Anthony, Luis Fonsi, Gente de Zona, Ozuma + search 'Salsa Remix'.
