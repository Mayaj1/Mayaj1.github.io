About

At Guernsey Salsa we offer dance classes in Salsa and Batchata. There is a place for dancers of all levels from absolute beginners to those with experiance.

Classes are held on Mondays, Tuesdays and Wednesdays on the second floor of the Albion, and are taught by Heidi Alemonte. Private lessons are also available by arrangement.
